## About me

I like building things that solve actual problems.

Most of the time, that means backend services, AI workflows, small automations, Telegram bots, or some self-hosted setup that starts as "let me try this quickly" and somehow becomes part of my daily life.

I am especially interested in applied AI; where it fits into real workflows: task management, knowledge bases, reminders, internal tools, forms, workplace processes, and all the messy parts around them. 

Outside of that, I spend a lot of time on my homelab, cloud infrastructure, identity-aware access, and secure service exposure. I enjoy learning how systems are wired together, breaking them, fixing them, and breaking them again because I can.

Right now, I am working on stronger foundations in machine learning, data structures and algorithms, system design, and practical backend engineering.
